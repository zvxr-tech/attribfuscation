This is where you would place a consent form for the study.
It would contain information such as:

  • Name of Researcher, Faculty, Department, Telephone & Email
  • Sponsor (e.g. NSERC)
  • Purpose of the Study
  • What Will I Be Asked To Do?
  • What Type of Personal Information Will Be Collected?
  • Are there Risks or Benefits if I Participate?
  • What Happens to the Information I Provide?
  • Questions/Concerns Contact Information

Followed by a confirmation checkbox and buttons to proceed/cancel/print:

By checking this box,
        1) You understand to your satisfaction the information provided to you about your participation in this research project, and
        2) you agree to participate in the research project.
